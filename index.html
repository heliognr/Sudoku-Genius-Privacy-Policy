Privacy Policy

Sudoku Genius does not collect any personal data or require user sign-in.

Local Storage

Your game progress, settings, and high scores are saved locally on your device. We do not store or transmit this data.

Internet Usage

The "Hint" feature uses an AI model via Google (Genkit) and requires an internet connection. No personal data is sent—only the current puzzle state is processed temporarily to provide a suggestion.

Offline Use

Sudoku Genius is fully functional offline, except for the AI Hint feature.

Third-Party Services

We do not use analytics, ads, or any third-party trackers.
